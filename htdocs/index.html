An Overview

ALL AROUND THE WORLD WE ARE EXPERIENCING A RAPID DECLINE OF OUR MARINE ECOSYSTEMS. DUE TO UNSUSTAINABLE AND OFTEN ILLEGAL FISHING PRACTICES, THE PRESSURE ON MARINE LIFE IS SET FOR DISASTER. ESPECIALLY IN CAMBODIA; WHERE ENVIRONMENTAL PRESSURE, A LACK OF LEGAL ENFORCEMENT AND SCARCITY OF RESOURCES ARE ADDED TO THE TEST, THERE IS A DIRECT NEED FOR ACTION. SO IT IS HERE THAT WE WILL DO WHAT WE DO BEST… EMBRACE.

WE AIM TO FIND SOLUTIONS IN AN EMBRACE BETWEEN MAN AND NATURE, SO THAT A SUSTAINABLE SYMBIOSIS CAN BE REESTABLISHED.

AT EMBRACE WE STAND FOR OUR CONSEQUENCES. WE AIM TO DIVE DEEPER INTO ORIGINS OF THE PROBLEM TO PROVIDE SUPPORT AT THE BASE. WE BELIEVE IN A EVER POSITIVE – WHOLISTIC APPROACH.

SO, WHAT IS THE REAL PROBLEM OF CAMBODIA’S MARINE LIFE? FOR US, THE PROBLEM IS NOT THE ILLEGAL FISHING PRACTICES, NEITHER CAN WE BLAME THE UNSATISFIABLE MARKET FOR FISH, AND WE DEFINITELY DON’T JUST BLAME THE ENVIRONMENT EITHER. FOR SURE THEY ARE VIABLE CAUSES… BUT NOT THE REASON! OUR PROBLEM IS THE SCARCITY PRINCIPLE. SCARCITY LIMITS THE ACCESSIBILITY TO BETTER SOLUTIONS. IT DRIVES THE FISHERMEN TO FISH ILLEGALLY, IT LIMITS US TO INVEST IN PROTECTED AREAS AND IT CREATES A HUNGRY MARKET. IN FACT, IT IS THE BASE OF ALL OUR CONCERNS.

BUT, LUCKILY, OUR OCEANS CONTAIN A VASTLY ABUNDANT POTENTIAL. WITH THE RIGHT MOTIVATION, WE CAN REDISCOVER THE TREASURES OF THE SEA. BALD IDEAS WILL ALLOW OUR LOCAL HEROES TO EXPLORE A SUSTAINABLE WAY OF LIVING. WE WILL USE NATURE TO SOLVE NATURE’S PROBLEMS AND ALLOW FISHERMAN TO COLLABORATE IN THE PROCESS.

WANT TO KNOW WHAT EXACTLY THAT ENTAILS? WE INVITE YOU TO JOIN OUR EMBRACE AND DISCOVER KOH KARANG.
