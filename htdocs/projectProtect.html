MOU

Our collaboration with the Cambodian governement is based on a Memorandum Of Understanding (MOU). This legal framework provides us with a guarantee of operation- al rights over Koh Karang. The MOU is a collaboration be- tween the Cambodian Governement and Knai Bang Chatt with Marine Conservation Cambodia as an operation part- ner. Together we form a professional platform that consists of three essential components: commercial, non-govern- mental and governmental. This provides us with the inte- grated complete perspective needed for developing a sus- tainable platform. Esentially the MOU is the political base of the project that a window in time to reestablish a thriving eco-system.

GOALS OF THE MOU

  • Protection and enhancement of marine life
  • Income generation for fishermen
  • Promotion of sustainable tourism
  • Development of education and awareness on the preservation of environment and
  • Establishment of technology for developing other Cambodian coastal areas

MARINE CONSERVATION CAMBODIA

Embrace Cambodia is the operational body (NGO), that has been formed exclusively for the development of the Marine National Park on Koh Karang. The creation of the masterplan, aswel as the execution of it will be led by our departments. Yet, as a young organisation, we rely on highly motivated individuals and supporting partner organisations such as Marine Conservation Cambodia, FFI and Song Saa Foundation for consulting and operations. Our goal is to establish a strong support network to enable the project to grow.

KNAI BANG CHATT

Since its opening in 2006, Knai Bang Chatt prides itself on its signature blend of privacy and personalized hospitality service. It's a unique seaside destination that provides a home for both tourist and employee. With their golden Green Growth 2050 standard, they have become a role model in sustainability. Their filosophy represents a highly respectfull approach to humanity, the environment and business. Their efforts to protect the nature around the resort, are inherently linked to purpose of our project.

MINISTERY OF ENVIRONMENT

The Ministery of Environment forms an esential part of the Cambodian Government, representing the rights and values of the natural environment throughout the country. Their Ministry consists out of wide aray of departements covering different environmental topics from coastal zones to forest areas. Their political position provides us with the legal ground required to develop a Marine National Park. We aim to work closely with their representatives in order to safeguard viability of the project.
